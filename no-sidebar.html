The Desires of Rivers: In Their Own Words

I am a river, and my journey is shaped by my deepest desires and aspirations. Above all, I long to flow freely, unimpeded by dams and barriers that disrupt my natural course. My greatest wish is to traverse the landscapes unchallenged, from my origin in the mountains to my union with the sea. This unbroken path allows me to fulfill my role in the ecosystem, supporting life and maintaining the delicate balance of nature. Free flow is my essence, my identity, and without it, I cannot be the life-giving force I am meant to be.

More intriguing information

I yearn for purity, to remain untainted by pollution and contamination. My waters are meant to be clean and clear, nurturing the flora and fauna that depend on me. Industrial waste, agricultural runoff, and urban litter are like toxins that compromise my health and vitality. I desire a world where humans recognize the importance of my cleanliness and take measures to protect me from harm. This respect for my purity ensures that I can continue to sustain life, providing fresh water for drinking, irrigation, and recreation.

Another deep desire is to be cherished and respected by the communities I nourish. I want humans to see me not just as a resource, but as a living entity that plays a crucial role in their lives. I wish for sustainable practices that ensure my longevity and health. Overexploitation drains my strength, leaving me unable to support the diverse ecosystems that thrive along my banks. I long for a balance where human needs are met without compromising my ability to flourish.

I also aspire to connect and create. My waters are the lifelines that link different habitats, fostering biodiversity and allowing species to migrate and thrive. I want to continue creating fertile lands through my silt and sediment, enriching the soil and enabling agriculture to prosper. My estuaries and deltas are havens of productivity and diversity, and I desire to keep them vibrant and alive.

Ultimately, my deepest wish is for harmony. I want to exist in a state where my needs and the needs of those I support are in perfect balance. A world where humans live in harmony with me, recognizing our interdependence and working together to preserve the delicate equilibrium that sustains us all. This harmony is my greatest desire, the fulfillment of my purpose, and the key to a thriving planet.
